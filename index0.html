About IPL

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, officially known as the Vivo IPL due to sponsorship reasons. It was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2008. IPL features franchises representing various cities and regions across India. The league has grown in popularity worldwide and features some of the best cricket players from around the globe.
